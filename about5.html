FRIENDS OF IFCE

Friends of IFCE is a network of people and corporations which support IFCE’s mission. For a $20 donation, you will be contributing to environmental improvements, species saving and civil society development in China.

Benefits include:

  • ● Weekly environmental/energy news
  • ● IFCE events invitation
  • ● Meetings with visiting delegations from China
  • ● Business/partner introduction in China
  • ● Network with people who are interested in environment and conservation

Please email ifce-adm@ifce.org to join IFCE community!
